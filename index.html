About Tab Codes

A Wee Bit About Me

Hi! I'm the "Tab" in "Tab Codes". I'm a highly creative Web Developer looking for an entry level position with a company that is seeking an indivual that is dependable, organized yet playful with design.

I've always been a creative soul but originally career paths that stifled that. Although it is very rewarding in helping others, the opportunities for me to express my creativeness was limited. Enter Web Development.

I started dabbling with coding and challenging myself to develop sites that functioned as well as looked cohesive. Spending a year just kind of tinkering online following lessons, listening to podcasts and clicking like a mad woman to absorb as much as I could!

Often I am told that it's not the Web Developers task to design the site. I disagree. Ya, I know that UI/UX knowledge is important and specific. I am very interested in continuing my learning in that area. My goal is to become a Full Front End Web Developer well versed in languages and design.
