Post Rows Grrr...

Why I hate Post Rows

I'll tell you why... S p i d e r M i t e s. These nasty tenatious little buggers always show up more often then not, in a post row. They tend to lay dormant under the plastic on the floor or up high in the crevices of the posts.

They start showing themselves around week three of a crop. You might be excited that you catch them in a spot no bigger than the size of your finger tip, don't be. For even though you douse the little buggers with predatory mites, the very next week they will have multiplied exponentially. From there on it's like playing tag with two people. Never ending.
